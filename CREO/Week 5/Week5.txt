Why are cosmetic thread used when modelling parts in CREO?

	Makes digital sketches clearer for fabricators, easier to implement. Physical threads only really needed for stress simulations

Why might you use the hole tool when creating a part in CREO?

	Provides finer hole control i.e. countersink, counterbore, drilled, standard iso sizing etc.
